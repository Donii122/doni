What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store stateful information, enable features like shopping carts, and provide insights into site usage.

Types of cookies we use

  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site so we can improve the experience.
  • Marketing: Optional cookies used for tailored promotions and advertising.
