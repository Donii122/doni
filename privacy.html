At BeniChem we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains the types of personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

Information we collect

  • Account & contact data: name, email, address, phone (when provided).
  • Order & payment data: purchase details and billing information needed to fulfil orders (payments are processed by third-party providers).
  • Technical & usage data: IP address, device, browser, pages visited, and performance data collected via cookies and analytics tools.

How we use your data

We use data to process orders, communicate with you, provide customer support, prevent fraud, and to improve our site and services. We only process data where we have a lawful basis (for example, contract performance, legitimate interest, or with your consent).

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies for necessary site operations and, where you consent, for analytics and marketing. Manage cookie preferences via the cookie banner or by clicking the "Manage cookie settings" button above. For full details see our Cookie Policy.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, port, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise any rights, contact us at privacy@benichem.example. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

Sharing and disclosures

We share data only with trusted service providers (payment processors, shipping partners, analytics). We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and for legitimate business purposes (for example, record-keeping and fraud prevention).
